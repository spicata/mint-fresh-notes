# At Risk for HIV

- People disproportionally affected by HIV
	- Men who have sex with men;
	- Ethnic minorities;
	- Women;
	- Young people (Teenagers and young adults); and
	- People who inject drugs.
- Those who are unable to get treatment develop into AIDS.
